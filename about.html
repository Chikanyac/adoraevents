Adora Events was born in Harare — Room 105, 1st Floor Sahil Building, Robert Mugabe Rd / Angwa Street — with a simple idea: food should be intentional, tables should breathe, and light should be kind to skin.

Led by Ethel, we cater weddings, lobola, lobo-weddings, funerals, corporate functions and private dinners. Every menu is farm-sourced, every table is designed for conversation, every service is quiet and precise.
