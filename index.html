Philosophy

Adora is built on restraint. Quiet tables, considered portions, light that flatters skin.

We believe a great celebration doesn’t shout — it stays, like perfume on linen. Every celebration is edited like a film — light, texture, taste and ritual composed to linger.

From Borrowdale garden weddings to Meikles corporate galas, our work is the same: intentional, warm, and timeless.
